About Me

Welcome to my personal website! I am a spatial data analyst passionate about remote sensing and urban sustainability.

I am a GIS analyst and geospatial researcher with expertise in spatial analysis, remote sensing, and urban development. I have contributed to diverse projects across multiple countries, leveraging geospatial technologies to address urban planning, environmental sustainability, and transportation challenges.

I have worked as a researcher at the Carinthia University of Applied Sciences (Austria) and the University of New Mexico (USA), applying remote sensing techniques to study Urban Heat Islands (UHI) and developing real-time geo-visualization systems for bicycle station mapping. I also contributed to the national geolocation and navigation system of Iran as a spatial data analyst in Tehran. Additionally, I have worked as an urban development engineer overseeing construction projects.

My technical expertise includes Google Earth Engine (GEE), Python, JavaScript, PostgreSQL, Node.js, and Leaflet, as well as GIS platforms such as ArcGIS. I specialize in geospatial data processing, remote sensing analysis, and web-based mapping applications.

I am passionate about applying GIS and remote sensing to real-world problems, particularly in the fields of urban sustainability, environmental monitoring, and transportation planning.
